Athletics

Forming Good Men & Good Sportsmen

At other schools, athletics can be an exclusive pursuit, reserved for the elite. And while we pride ourselves on the 56 Conference Championships and 185 All-Americans we’ve produced, we’re also proud of how many of our students don’t just spectate, they participate. Over a third of our student body is part of intercollegiate sports, intramurals and clubs. Enjoy competition, camaraderie and grow in your sportsmanship at H-SC.
